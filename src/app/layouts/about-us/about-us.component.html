Our benefits

HEALTHCARE

Dream Makers will receive a premium healthcare assurance which is valued as 40 million VND and are offered free health check-up and treatments at leading clinics and hospitals. At Navigos Group, we always appreciate spirit of work-life balance through attractive policies including sponsored clubs such as badminton, volleyball,…; salary pay in advance when necessary…

PAID LEAVE

Besides national holidays and Tet holiday, Dream Makers have 15 annual paid leaves, 06 sick-leaves (without doctor’s certifying paper) and 05 paid leaves for emergency cases.

TRAINING

At Navigos Group, we offer our Dream Makers with various training programs and mentorship programs based on their desired career plan at "Naviworks University".

AWARDS

We always appreciate the dedication from Dream Makers by valuable recognition and rewarding programs such as Spirit Award, Service Hero, Service Award… Varying from different job positions, some awards like Best Performance, Best Sales are also handed Dream Makers in order to encourage their ‘Improve it!’ and ‘Own it!’ spirit.

TEAM ACTIVITIES

Many teambuilding activities are organized every month which give Dream Makers chances to have fun, to relax and connect with colleagues: Snack Time, Women's Day, Christmas, Company Trip, Company Birthday, Japanese culture exchange, Olympic, Year End Party,...

PANTRY

Spacious and gorgeous pantry is equipped with fridges, microwaves, coffee machine, vending machine,… Dream Makers also can enjoy free tea and coffee at the pantry in their working hours to relax.
